Hope Begins with One

The One Child movement is rooted in a simple but powerful belief: that changing the life of one child can echo far beyond what we see. Unathi is one of those children—his life touched by the care, commitment, and generosity of people who chose to make a difference. His story mirrors countless others: real stories, filled with colour, resilience, and quiet courage.

Meet Unathi

A child who makes you smile without even trying. He loves running around barefoot, drawing with broken crayons, and asking many many questions.

Unathi comes from a place where things aren’t always easy. There are days when food runs low and grown-ups have to make tough choices. But what’s interesting is Unathi isn’t defined by that. What stands out about him isn’t what he lacks—it’s everything he is.

Unforgettable — a face full of hope and joy.

OneChild, through Holanathi, delivers real impact across South African communities
